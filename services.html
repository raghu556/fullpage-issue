Leveraging the Legacy

Digital data and processes exist in most of the enterprises we serve today. The cost of replacing legacy systems is significant. eTouch’s digital engineering approach suggests ways of leveraging existing systems using technologies such as Business Process Management (BPM), Enterprise Application Integration (EAI) and Service Oriented Architecture (SOA). We build APIs, wrappers, and adapters for legacy applications to unlock the data potential of legacy systems.

Digital Web Engineering for Stakeholder Collaboration

Enterprises today have several stakeholders — customers, employees, business partners, vendors, management, and citizens. eTouch’s engineering teams understand this ecosystem of collaborators, as well as business process nuances, to propose the most optimal solution path for present and future scalability. We have expertise in building systems that bring data and process access to collaborators via websites, portals, content management systems, enterprise search solutions, collaboration platforms, and knowledge management systems.

We bootstrap the development processes for these solutions using our accelerators and solution frameworks in multiple languages such as Java, C#, Python, PHP, Ruby, and JavaScript.

Digital Enterprise Security

Our digital engineering approach always considers the security aspect for our digital solutions. Our services portfolio comprises security assessment, security design and architecture, security policy and process consulting, security solution implementation, security testing, and security audit.

Our teams have deep expertise in using tools for Access Management, Identity Management, Authentication and Authorization Services, Single Sign-On, Directory Services, Intrusion Detection, and Penetration Testing.

Testing Services for the Digital Enterprise

We offer a complete range of testing services that help enterprises to not only provide reliable and accurate data and services, but also to reduce the associated investments. We offer, along with our testing services, accelerators (including our proprietary eTAAP) that enable test automation using a range of open source frameworks such as Jenkins, Selenium, TestNG, and Continuous Integration tools such as Maven. Our testing approach covers all digital platforms, web and mobile transactions, as well as analytics services.

Apart from functional testing, system testing, regression testing, and API testing services, we provide specialized services such as performance testing, security testing, and multivariate testing to provide insights that better serve the needs of stakeholders as well as application and service users.

We help set up testing Centers of Excellence (CoE) for enterprises, and provide a roadmap for adoption of testing automation.
